Prohibited acts

Acts of vandalism

・Destroying or disabling another player's modeling with malicious intent.
・Using a block with a lock function for purposes other than theft prevention.
・Locking a shared chest that has already been unlocked.
・Killing other players with malicious intent.
・Chatting repeatedly.
・Ranting or r18 chat.

Not overloading the server

・Create redstone circuits with too much load
・Hoard too many mobs (trap towers are free)
・Dos / ddos attacks
・Join with two accounts at the same time (Java and BE at the same time is also prohibited)

Illegality

Use of mods, clients, or textures that give you an unfair advantage.
(Mods such as PvP clients, OptiFine (including shaders), minimaps, recipes, inventory organizers, etc. are not prohibited)
