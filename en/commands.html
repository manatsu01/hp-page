Allowed Commands


The following commands are mainly allowed to be used.

Main

/cp res　　　Open Resource world
/spawn　　　Tp to spawn
/oldspawn　　　Tp to oldspawn

Vanilla commands

/tell [player-name] [message]
/seed　　Check seed of the world.

LWC(chest lock)

--Once a chest is placed, only the player who placed it will be able to open it!
After entering a command, right-click on a block or door that can hold items such as chests, etc.
/lock　　　Lock Storage.
/unlock　　　Unlock Storage.
/cprivate [Username]　　　Share the storage with [Username]

GSit

--Can Sit
/gsit -- Sit.
/glay -- Lay
/gcrawl -- Crawl
/gspin -- Spin
